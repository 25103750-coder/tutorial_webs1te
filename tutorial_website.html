Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

Shrimp Fried Rice is a quick and easy dish made with rice, shrimp, vegetables, and savory seasonings. It is perfect for lunch or dinner and can be cooked in under 30 minutes. This recipe is beginner-friendly and requires simple ingredients.

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2-3 people

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold rice)
  • 200 grams shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions (optional for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion. Cook until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes until pink.
  4. Push shrimp to the side and crack egg into pan.
  5. Scramble the egg.
  6. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  8. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce.
  9. Mix everything evenly.
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  12. Serve hot.

Tips

  • Use cold rice for best texture.
  • Do not overcook shrimp.
  • You can add chicken or other protein.

Serving Suggestions

Serve hot with lemon, chili sauce, or extra soy sauce.

Chili Con Carne

Description

Chili Con Carne is a hearty, spicy stew made with ground beef, beans, tomatoes, and chili peppers. It’s warm, filling, and perfect for a cozy meal. You can adjust the spice level to your liking, making it great for beginners and chili lovers alike.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can kidney beans (400g), drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (400g)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1–2 tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 cup beef broth or water

Equipment

  • Large pot or deep pan
  • Wooden spoon
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Can opener

Procedure (Step-by-Step)

  1. Chop onion and bell pepper. Mince garlic. Drain kidney beans.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and garlic. Cook until soft (about 2 minutes).
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in bell pepper and cook 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add chili powder, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  7. Pour in diced tomatoes and kidney beans.
  8. Add beef broth or water and bring to a boil.
  9. Lower heat and simmer 20–30 minutes.
  10. Taste and adjust seasoning.

Cooking Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4–5 people

Difficulty: Easy

Beginner Tips

  • Simmer longer for thicker chili.
  • Add chili powder slowly if you want it spicy.
  • If too thick, add a little water.

Serving Suggestion

Serve with rice, tortilla chips, or bread. Top with shredded cheese or sour cream if desired.

Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is a crispy breaded chicken breast topped with rich tomato sauce and melted cheese. It’s golden outside, juicy inside, and cheesy on top. Served with pasta or salad, it’s a comforting and satisfying main dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts (cut in half lengthwise)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (optional)
  • 1 cup marinara or tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup cooking oil (for frying)
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional garnish)

Equipment

  • Frying pan
  • Baking dish
  • Tongs
  • Bowls (for breading)
  • Oven

Procedure (Step-by-Step)

  1. Slice chicken breasts in half lengthwise and pat dry.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  3. Prepare breading station (flour, egg, breadcrumbs + Parmesan).
  4. Dip chicken in flour → egg → breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat oil in pan over medium heat.
  6. Fry 3–4 minutes per side until golden brown.
  7. Place in baking dish. Add sauce and mozzarella.
  8. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15–20 minutes.
  9. Garnish with basil or parsley.

Cooking Information

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 25–30 minutes

Servings: 3–4 people

Difficulty: Medium

Beginner Tips

  • Make sure oil is hot before frying.
  • Do not overcrowd the pan.
  • Chicken is done when internal temperature reaches 75°C.

Serving Suggestion

Serve with spaghetti, garlic bread, or fresh salad.

Sinigang na Isda

Description

Sinigang na Isda is a classic Filipino sour soup made with fresh fish, tamarind (or sinigang mix), and vegetables like kangkong, radish, and eggplant. It’s tangy, comforting, and perfect for rainy days.

Ingredients

  • 500g fish (tilapia, bangus, or any white fish)
  • 1 medium tomato, quartered
  • 1 small onion, quartered
  • 1 cup radish, sliced
  • 1 cup kangkong (water spinach)
  • 1 small eggplant, sliced
  • 1–2 tbsp fish sauce (patis)
  • 1 pack sinigang mix or fresh tamarind juice
  • 6 cups water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Equipment

  • Large pot
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Ladle

Procedure (Step-by-Step)

  1. Slice vegetables and clean fish.
  2. Bring 6 cups of water to a boil.
  3. Add onion and tomato. Boil 3–5 minutes.
  4. Add radish and eggplant. Cook 5 minutes.
  5. Add fish carefully. Simmer 5–7 minutes.
  6. Add sinigang mix or tamarind juice.
  7. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
  8. Add kangkong last. Cook 1–2 minutes.

Cooking Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15–20 minutes

Servings: 3–4 people

Difficulty: Easy

Beginner Tips

  • Add fish after vegetables to prevent overcooking.
  • Taste before adding more sinigang mix.
  • Do not overboil or fish will break apart.

Serving Suggestion

Best served hot with steamed rice.

Beef Tacos with Lettuce and Tomato

Description

Beef Tacos with Lettuce and Tomato are a fun and easy Mexican-inspired meal. Juicy, seasoned ground beef is tucked into a tortilla, topped with fresh lettuce and tomato. It’s flavorful, customizable, and perfect for any time.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 6–8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1–2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 small onion (optional)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • ½ cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • Salsa or sour cream (optional)

Equipment

  • Frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Serving plate

Procedure (Step-by-Step)

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Cook garlic and onion in oil.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add seasoning and cook 2–3 minutes.
  5. Warm tortillas.
  6. Fill tortillas with beef and toppings.
  7. Serve immediately.

Cooking Information

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 3–4 people

Difficulty: Easy

Beginner Tips

  • Drain excess oil if too greasy.
  • Do not overfill tortillas.
  • Adjust spice level as preferred.

Serving Suggestion

Serve with nachos, salsa, or lime wedges.

Scrambled Eggs with Toast and Tomato

Description

Scrambled Eggs with Toast and Tomato is a simple and nutritious meal that is perfect for beginners. It is quick to prepare and provides a good balance of protein, carbohydrates, and fresh vegetables. This dish is commonly enjoyed for breakfast but can also be served as a light lunch or dinner.

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 2 people

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 slices bread
  • 1 medium tomato (sliced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them well.
  2. Add milk, salt, and pepper and mix together.
  3. Heat butter in a pan over medium-low heat.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  5. Stir gently with a spatula while the eggs cook.
  6. Continue stirring until the eggs become soft and fluffy.
  7. Toast the bread slices in a toaster or pan.
  8. Place scrambled eggs on a plate with toasted bread.
  9. Add fresh tomato slices on the side and serve.

Tips

  • Cook the eggs on low heat to keep them soft and creamy.
  • Do not overcook the eggs because they can become dry.
  • You can add cheese or herbs for extra flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Serve warm with toasted bread and fresh tomato slices.

Chicken Vegetable Stir-Fry with Rice

Description

Chicken Vegetable Stir-Fry with Rice is a quick and healthy dish made with chicken, fresh vegetables, and savory seasonings. It is a beginner-friendly recipe that can be cooked in less than 30 minutes.

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2–3 people

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 200 grams chicken breast (cut into small pieces)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions (optional for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook for about 4–5 minutes.
  4. Add carrots and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add broccoli and bell peppers and stir-fry for another 3 minutes.
  6. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce and mix well.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Cook for another 2–3 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  9. Serve the stir-fry over warm rice.

Tips

  • Cut the chicken into small pieces so it cooks faster.
  • Do not overcook the vegetables to keep them slightly crunchy.
  • You can add mushrooms or cabbage for more vegetables.

Serving Suggestions

Serve hot with steamed rice.

Tuna Sandwich with Lettuce and Tomato

Description

Tuna Sandwich with Lettuce and Tomato is a simple and healthy meal made with canned tuna, fresh vegetables, and soft bread. It is easy to prepare and perfect for beginners. This sandwich is great for lunch, snacks, or a quick dinner.

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 2 people

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna (drained)
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 slices bread
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 1 medium tomato (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional)

Instructions

  1. Drain the tuna and place it in a bowl.
  2. Add mayonnaise and black pepper.
  3. Mix well until combined.
  4. Spread butter on the bread slices if desired.
  5. Spread the tuna mixture on two slices of bread.
  6. Add lettuce leaves and tomato slices on top.
  7. Cover with the remaining bread slices.
  8. Cut the sandwiches in half and serve.

Tips

  • Drain the tuna well to prevent the sandwich from becoming soggy.
  • You can toast the bread for a crispier sandwich.
  • Add cucumber or cheese for extra flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Serve the tuna sandwich with chips, salad, or fresh fruit for a complete meal.

Vegetable Omelette with Bread

Description

Vegetable Omelette with Bread is a quick and nutritious meal made with eggs, fresh vegetables, and toasted bread. It is a beginner-friendly dish that can be prepared in less than 15 minutes. This meal is perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a light dinner.

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 8 minutes

Total Time: 13 minutes

Servings: 2 people

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup carrots (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup onions (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil or butter
  • 4 slices bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions (optional for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them well.
  2. Add chopped carrots, bell peppers, and onions.
  3. Add salt and pepper and mix well.
  4. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  6. Cook for 2–3 minutes until the bottom is set.
  7. Flip the omelette carefully and cook the other side for 1–2 minutes.
  8. Toast the bread slices in a toaster or pan.
  9. Serve the vegetable omelette with toasted bread.

Tips

  • Chop vegetables into small pieces so they cook quickly.
  • Cook on medium heat to avoid burning the omelette.
  • You can add cheese or ham for extra flavor.

Serving Suggestions

Serve hot with toasted bread. You can also add ketchup, chili sauce, or a side of fresh fruit.

Garlic Butter Shrimp with Rice and Broccoli

Description

Garlic Butter Shrimp with Rice and Broccoli is a flavorful and quick meal made with shrimp cooked in a rich garlic butter sauce, served with rice and steamed broccoli. This dish is beginner-friendly and can be prepared in less than 30 minutes. It is a balanced meal with protein, carbohydrates, and vegetables.

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2–3 people

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 200 grams shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parsley or spring onions (optional for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Steam or boil the broccoli for about 5 minutes until tender. Set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add the shrimp and cook for 2–3 minutes until they turn pink.
  5. Add butter and stir until melted.
  6. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Cook for another 1–2 minutes while stirring.
  8. Serve the garlic butter shrimp over warm rice.
  9. Place steamed broccoli on the side and serve immediately.

Tips

  • Do not overcook the shrimp because they can become tough.
  • Use fresh garlic for better flavor.
  • You can add chili flakes for a little spice.

Serving Suggestions

Serve hot with lemon wedges or extra butter sauce over the rice.
